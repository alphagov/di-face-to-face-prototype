Set up an authenticator app

1. Open your authenticator app on your smartphone, tablet or computer.

I don't have an authenticator app

You can download an authenticator app for your smartphone or tablet from the app store.

If you don't have a smartphone or tablet, search online for an authenticator app for your computer.

You can use any authenticator app.
