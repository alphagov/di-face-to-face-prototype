Prove your identity online using one of the following photo ID documents

UK photocard driving licence

You can use a valid full or provisional driving licence with your photo on it.

Biometric passport (or 'ePassport')

Biometric passports allow you to go through eGates at airpots. All valid UK passports are biometric passports but not all non-UK passports are. To check if you have a biometric passport, look for a small rectangular symbol on the front cover.

If your UK passport has expired, you can still use it to prove your identity up until 18 months after the expiry date.

Biometric Residence Permit (BRP)

You can use a valid BRP to prove your identity. Your BRP holds your personal details, photo and fingerprints if you are a UK visa holder. It shows your immigration status and entitlements while you remain in the United Kingdom.

What if I cannot prove my identity online?
If you cannot prove your identity online, you can take these documents to the Post Office to get them checked in person. You'll need to enter your details on GOV.UK before you can do this.
