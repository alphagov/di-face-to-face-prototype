You can prove your identity online using the following photo ID documents

UK photocard driving licence

You'll need a valid full or provisional driving licence with your photo on it.

UK or non-UK biometric passport

Biometric passports (or 'ePassports') have a small rectangular symbol on the front cover. You can use them to go through eGates at airpots and train stations.

Biometric Residence Permit (BRP)

An identity card that holds a migrant's personal details, photo and fingerprints. It shows their immigration status and entitlements while they remain in the United Kingdom.

If you can't prove your identity online, you can take these documents into the Post Office to get them checked in person.
