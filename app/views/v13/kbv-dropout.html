Enter the details from your photo ID and answer security questions

To prove your identity, you'll need to:

  • enter the details from your UK driving licence or UK passport (you might find it easier to do this if you have the photo ID you want to use with you)
  • enter your current home address (and your previous address if you've recently moved)
  • answer some questions that only you should know the answers to
