How do you want to prove your identity?

You can prove your identity online or at a Post Office. The quickest way to prove your identity is online - this takes around 10 minutes.

Prove your identity online

You’ll need to scan your photo ID and take a photo of your face with your selfie camera using a GOV.UK app. If you cannot use an app, you can still prove your identity online by answering some security questions.

Prove your identity at a Post Office

You’ll need to enter some details on GOV.UK and then take your chosen photo ID to a Post Office. A member of staff will scan your photo ID and take a photo of you. You’ll get an email with the outcome of your identity check - usually within a day of visiting the Post Office.
