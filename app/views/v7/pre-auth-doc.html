Tell us if you have one of the following types of photo ID

This will help us find the best way for you to prove your identity.

UK photocard driving licence

You can use a UK driving licence if it:

  • has your photo
  • has not expired

It can be a full or provisional UK driving licence.

UK passport

You can use any UK passport. If your UK passport has expired, you can still use it to prove your identity up until 18 months after its expiry date.

Non-UK passport with a biometric chip

You can use a non-UK passport if it:

  • has a biometric chip
  • has not expired

To check if your passport has a biometric chip, look for the rectangular biometric chip symbol on the front cover.

UK biometric residence permit

You can use a UK biometric residence permit (BRP) that has not expired.
