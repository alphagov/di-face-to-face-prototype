About GOV.UK One Login accounts

GOV.UK One Login accounts are new. At the moment you can only use your GOV.UK One Login account with a few services.

GOV.UK One Login accounts are currently separate from other government accounts (for example Government Gateway or Universal Credit).

In the future, you'll be able to use your GOV.UK One Login account to sign in to most services on GOV.UK.
