You can prove your identity at a Post Office if you have one of the following documents

You can take your photo ID to a Post Office to be checked in person. You'll need to enter your details on GOV.UK before you can do this.

EU photocard driving licence

You can use a valid full or provisional driving licence with your photo on it.

Non-biometric passport

You can use any type of passport to prove your identity in person at the Post Office, not just biometric passports.

National identity card from a European Economic Area (EEA) country

You can use a national identity card from countries in the European Economic Area (EEA) except Denmark, Iceland and Ireland.

You can also use one of the documents listed on the previous page to prove your identity in person at the Post Office.
