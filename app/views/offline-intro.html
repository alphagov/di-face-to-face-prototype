Proving your identity in person

What you need to do

  1. Complete an online form and select a photo ID you can take to a Post Office. Then we’ll send you a confirmation email.

  2. Take the confirmation email and your photo ID to a Post Office.

  3. Post Office staff will check your photo ID and take a photo of your face to confirm your identity.

Not all Post Offices offer this service. You can find your nearest Post Office that offers in branch verification here (opens in new tab).

After you've visited the Post Office

We’ll email you with the outcome of your identity check. If you’ve successfully proved your identity, sign into your GOV.UK One Login to access the service you need.
