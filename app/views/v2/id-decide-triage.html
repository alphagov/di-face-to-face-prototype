We need to ask some questions to decide how to prove your identity

You must have at least one of these documents to prove your identity through this service:

  • UK passport
  • Other passport
  • Biometric residence permit (BRP)
  • UK driving licence

I do not have any of these documents


It will take you around 15 minutes to prove your identity online. You can do this through an app. If you are not able to use the app, we can check your UK passport details and ask some questions that only you will know.

We will first ask you some questions to help decide which option is right for you.

I want to prove my identity another way

If you are not able to prove your identity online, you can prove your identity in person.

This process will take a lot longer than proving your identity online.

You will need to complete a short online form, and take your identity document to a Post Office.
