Which identity document can you take to a Post Office?

Select which document you have to hand and would be able to take to the Post Office within the next 10 days.

The document must:

  • be in your current name
  • be in your current address (where address is included)
  • include your date of birth (at least 1 document should include this)
  • be original documents (you cannot use photocopies)
