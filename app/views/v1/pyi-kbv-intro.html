Enter your passport details and answer security questions

We can check your passport details and ask you some questions that only you will know. This will help us protect you against online identity theft.

  • your UK passport details (including your name, passport number and the date your passport expires)
  • your current home address (you might also need your previous address if you've recently moved)

We'll then ask you some questions that only you should know the answers to.

The questions will be about things like your mobile phone contract, bank account, and any credit cards, loans or mortgages you have. Answering these questions will help us stop anyone else who might have your details from pretending to be you.

We will not keep your answers.

! Warning Try to answer these questions as accurately as you can. You will not be able to change your answers if you make a mistake.
How we know what questions to ask you

We'll work with Experian (opens in new tab). to make sure we ask you questions that only you can answer. This is because companies like Experian have access to lots of information that we can check your answers against.

Read our privacy notice (opens in new tab) if you'd like to know more about how your details will be used to prove your identity.
