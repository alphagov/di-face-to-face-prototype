Prove your identity at a Post Office with one of the following types of photo ID

Based on what you told us, you cannot prove your identity online.

If you have any of the following types of photo ID, you can prove your identity at a Post Office instead.

European Union (EU) photocard driving licence

You can use an EU driving licence if:

  • it is a plastic photocard, not a paper or handwritten licence
  • it has not expired
  • the address on it is the same as your current address (if it has an address)

Non-UK passport

You can use a non-UK passport as long as it has not expired.

National identity card from a European Economic Area country

You can use a national identity card from a European Economic Area (EEA) country if:

  • it is a plastic photocard, not a paper or handwritten licence
  • it has not expired
  • the address on it is the same as your current address (if it has an address)

The EEA includes EU countries, Norway, Iceland and Liechtenstein.
