How to prove your identity in person

To prove your identity in person, you’ll need to:


  1. Choose a photo ID you can take to a Post Office.

  2. Complete an online form by entering your name, date of birth and current home address (and your previous address if you’ve recently moved).

  3. After entering your details, you’ll get a confirmation email with a customer letter attached from Post Office.

  4. Take your customer letter and photo ID to a Post Office within 10 days. A member of staff will scan your photo ID and take a photo of you.

Not all Post Offices offer this service. You can find your nearest Post Office that offers in branch verification here (opens in new tab).

After you've visited the Post Office

You’ll get an email with the outcome of your identity check - usually within a day of visiting the Post Office.
