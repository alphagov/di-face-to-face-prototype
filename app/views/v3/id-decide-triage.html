Ways to prove your identity

Prove your identity online

The quickest way to prove your identity is through downloading a GOV.UK smartphone app. If you cannot download a smartphone app, you can still prove your identity online by answering some security questions.

Prove your identity in person

You will need to take a photo ID to a Post Office. A member of staff will take a photo of your face to verify your identity. Once your identity has been proven, you must return online to your GOV.UK account to access the service you need.
