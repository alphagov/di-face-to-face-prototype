Proving your identity in person

What you need to do

  1. Complete an online form to provide your basic personal details and select a photo ID you can take to a Post Office. We will then send you a confirmation email.

  2. Take the confirmation email and your photo ID to a Post Office.

  3. A member of Post Office staff will process your photo ID and take a photo of your face to verify your identity.

Not all Post Offices offer this service. You can find your nearest Post Office that offers in branch verification here (opens in new tab).

What happens after the Post Office

Once your identity has been proven, you must return online to your GOV.UK account to access the service you need.
